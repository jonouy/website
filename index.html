About Me

Hi, my name is Jon and I want to make a difference in the world.

Growing up, we were all asked what we wanted to do in future. Like many kids, I had no idea, and it wasn't until the summer of 2013 that I've decided to pursue a career in business. The reason behind that was because I was waking up every morning doing what I loved and earning money for it. I was making money playing a mobile trading card game. It sounds hard to believe, but I have the Paypal transaction statements to prove it! However, that was not the reason why I got into business, but rather the fact that I was able to meet thousands of people across the world and built strong relationships that still stand to this day. This opened me up to the concept of technology and the impact it has on our soceity today.

So I decided to major in marketing as an attempt to share my experiences with other people around me. Little did I know, this would open up many opportunities for me. I came to the University of Ottawa and began my career as a residence mentor, then teaching assistant, and now an international coach. I was able to help many students succeed and become a mentor that I always wanted, and I was able to make a difference in someone's life. It was a wonderful feeling that I did not want to ever end, and it didn't.

I've taken this experience and entered the field of project management in order to make an impact on a larger scale. I was able to help many teams finish projects that displayed a visible outcome, and I can say "I helped with that!" However, I've never forgotten my love for technology as well, which is why this website exist. I've met an amazing visual designer and he's currently helping me build my technological skills (I finally got the mentor I've always wanted).

Right now I'm just excited to be learning how to build websites, design visual artwork, and face swapping on Photoshop. Who knows what's going to happen next. But stay connected with me and I'll keep you updated.

Ask me questions, because without questions, I can't give answers.
